SocialMet

SocialMet was a brave initiative by our university. We are not popular in many peoples eyes. We don't rank highly, we are losing a lot of jobs, we are in the front lines of higher education. Some people may not even view us as a 'proper' university. In fact, we are at the forefront of change and therefore a great guide of what is to come. So, when senior management agreed to open the doors to our community, it was brave. I'm honoured to have imagined it, built it and to look after it.
